Strategic Popoltastic

Brand Revision Explorer

This page presents accent color, typography, and motif options for the Strategic Popoltastic brand refresh. The brand serves Dutch mid-market companies with strategy consulting. We are revising the accent color (currently warm gold), font stack, and signature motif while retaining the dark navy primary and brand blue. Each section is numbered for easy reference in discussion.
