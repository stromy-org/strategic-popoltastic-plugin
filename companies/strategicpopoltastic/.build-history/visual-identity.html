Color Rules

  • Rule 1: Never use pure black (#000). Use Dark (#0A1628) instead.
  • Rule 2: Never use pure white (#FFF). Use Near-white (#F8F9FA) instead.
  • Rule 3: Ice blue accent is used sparingly — rules, highlights, the logo slash. Never as a full background except for small tags/badges.
  • Rule 4: Section divider slides use Dark (#0A1628) background with white text and ice blue accent.
  • Rule 5: Content slides use Near-white or Blue-50 backgrounds.
  • Rule 6: The ice-blue-on-dark combination is the signature brand pairing.
