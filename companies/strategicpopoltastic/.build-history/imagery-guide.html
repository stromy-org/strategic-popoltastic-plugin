01 / Philosophy

Imagery Philosophy

Strategic Popoltastic's imagery communicates authority, analytical precision, and the clarity of strategic insight. Every visual asset belongs in a boardroom-quality document — sophisticated, purposeful, and never decorative for its own sake.

Our imagery is the visual equivalent of sharp strategic thinking: precise compositions, controlled palettes, and subjects that suggest navigation, game theory, and market intelligence. Every image earns its place — no filler.

Two core metaphors anchor the visual language: Strategic Navigation (compasses, charts, cartography — "Read the market. Find your edge.") and Chess / Game Theory (boards, isolated pieces, game-in-progress — Popol's consulting world). Supporting themes of dark textures and geometric architecture complete the library.

02 / Visual Principles

Four Pillars of Visual Language

Subjects

Compasses, nautical charts, vintage cartography, chess pieces and boards, architectural geometry, dark stone and metal textures, horizon lines.

Avoid

Stock handshakes, people smiling at laptops, generic office interiors, abstract colored gradients, cliche light bulbs, puzzle pieces, low-res imagery.

Composition

Tight geometric crops. Strong leading lines. Rule of thirds or centered symmetry. Prefer 16:9 or 2:1 ratios. Negative space is a feature.

Treatment

Desaturated with brand overlay. High contrast, deep shadows. Cool undertone trending toward navy. Vignette for hero images. Always processed.

Treatment Profiles

Profile Saturation Contrast Overlay Vignette Use
Navigation (warm) -35% 1.12x Dark navy 12% Yes Hero images, section openers
Chess (dramatic) -50% 1.20x Dark navy 18% Yes Statement slides, covers
Textures (background) -45% 1.15x Dark navy 20% No Slide backgrounds, cards
Architecture (blue) -40% 1.18x Brand blue 15% Yes Professional contexts, headers

Sharp Processing Pipeline

1. Resize           1920px max width           maintain aspect ratio
2. Desaturate       -35% to -50%               pull color back, let structure dominate
3. Contrast          1.12x to 1.20x linear     sharpen tonal separation
4. Color overlay     #0A1628 or #1B3A5C        SVG multiply blend at 12-20% opacity
5. Vignette          radial gradient            optional — edges to dark at 40% opacity
6. Export            JPEG quality 85            optimized file size
