# Strategic Popoltastic — Brand Guidelines

Version 2.0 — March 2026

---

## 1. Brand Foundation

### Essence
Strategic precision with the confidence to own it.

### Positioning
Strategic Popoltastic delivers Bain/BCG-grade strategy audits, market positioning, and strategic roadmaps for Dutch mid-market and growth companies. We combine rigorous analytical frameworks with a direct, co-creative working style that cuts through corporate fog.

### Tagline
**Read the market. Find your edge.**

### Personality
| Trait | Expression |
|-------|-----------|
| Decisive | Clear recommendations, clear language. No hedging. |
| Intellectually confident | Deep frameworks, worn lightly. |
| Direct | Dutch directness as a consulting virtue. |
| Ambitious | Young founders, big-firm standards. |
| Sharp-witted | Serious work, delivered with personality. |

### Voice
| How we write | How we don't write |
|---|---|
| Short, declarative sentences | Long-winded consultant-speak |
| Active voice, present tense | Passive hedging |
| Frameworks named and explained simply | Jargon without context |
| Confident assertions backed by evidence | Vague claims |
| A touch of personality in tone | Stiff, interchangeable boilerplate |

### Language Strategy
Primary: Dutch (NL). Secondary: English (EN). Brand name works identically in both.

## 2. Logo

### Primary Lockup: The Edge
The logo uses a two-part wordmark separated by a diagonal ice blue slash:
- **"STRATEGIC"** — Work Sans, regular weight, wide letter-spacing, uppercase
- **Diagonal slash** — the brand motif, in accent ice blue (#7FB3D3), angled at approximately -8 degrees
- **"Popoltastic"** — Syne, bold weight (700), natural case

### Logo Variants
| Variant | Use case |
|---------|----------|
| Primary (dark bg) | STRATEGIC in neutral-400, ice blue slash, Popoltastic in white |
| Primary (light bg) | STRATEGIC in neutral-500, ice blue slash, Popoltastic in brand-dark |
| Primary (brand bg) | STRATEGIC in white 50%, ice blue slash, Popoltastic in white |
| With tagline | Primary lockup + "Read the market. Find your edge." below |
| Monochrome | All white or all dark — slash becomes same color at 60% opacity |
| Icon mark | S + slash + P in rounded square container |

### Color Rules by Background
| Background | STRATEGIC | Slash | Popoltastic |
|-----------|-----------|-------|-------------|
| Dark (#0A1628) | #8B949E | #7FB3D3 | #FFFFFF |
| Light (#F8F9FA) | #6E7681 | #5A9AB5 | #0A1628 |
| Brand (#1B3A5C) | rgba(255,255,255,0.5) | #7FB3D3 | #FFFFFF |

### Clear Space
Minimum clear space = height of the letter "P" in Popoltastic, on all four sides.

### Don'ts
- Never rotate the logo
- Never change the slash angle
- Never use the slash in a color other than ice blue/accent
- Never stack "Strategic" above "Popoltastic" without the slash
- Never use the wordmark below 120px wide

### Color Rules
1. Never use pure black (#000). Use Dark (#0A1628).
2. Never use pure white (#FFF). Use Near-white (#F8F9FA).
3. Ice blue accent is sparingly used — rules, highlights, the slash. Never a full background.
4. Section divider slides use Dark background + white text + ice blue accent.
5. Content slides use Near-white or Blue-50 backgrounds.
6. Ice-blue-on-dark is the signature brand combination.
7. Semantic colors are for functional use only — never as decorative brand elements.

## 5. Brand Motifs

### Primary Motif: The Edge

The diagonal ice blue slash from the logo extends into a system-wide design element.

#### Variants
| Variant | Implementation | Usage |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Edge line | 2.5px ice blue line, -8deg angle | Logo separator, section markers |
| Edge divider | Full-width hairline + ice blue slash at start | Horizontal dividers between sections |
| Edge accent | Short ice blue slash, standalone | Bullet replacement, decorative |
| Edge double | Two parallel slashes, second at 40% opacity | Premium emphasis, title accents |
| Edge fade | Slash with gradient fade-out | Subtle transitions, background decoration |
| Edge corner | L-shaped slash at container corner | Card/box accent, section openers |
| Edge border | Ice blue slash at top-left of container | Card/box accent |

#### CSS Classes
- `.edge-line` — inline slash via `::before`
- `.edge-divider` — full-width divider with slash accent
- `.edge-accent` — standalone short slash
- `.edge-accent--short` / `--tall` — size variants
- `.edge-accent--light` — for dark backgrounds

### Secondary Motif: The Chevron

A forward-pointing chevron (>) used as a complementary element for navigation, lists, and directional cues.

#### Variants
| Variant | Usage |
|---------|-------|
| Chevron bullet | Replaces standard bullets in key lists |
| Chevron nav | Navigation tab indicators, breadcrumbs |
| Chevron CTA | "Learn more >" style call-to-action links |
| Chevron section | Section transition indicator |

### Motif Usage Rules
1. The Edge is the primary brand element — use it for structural and decorative purposes.
2. The Chevron is secondary — use it for functional/navigational purposes.
3. Never combine Edge and Chevron in the same visual element.
4. Both motifs use the accent ice blue color by default.
5. On dark backgrounds, use the lighter accent variant (accent-200).

## 6. Applications

### Presentations (PPTX)
- Section dividers: Dark (#0A1628) bg, ice blue overline, Syne Bold title in white
- Content slides: Near-white bg, top navigation bar, Syne Bold titles, Work Sans body
- Navigation tabs: Work Sans, uppercase, active tab = Dark fill
- Page numbers: Space Mono, bottom-right
- Client logo: bottom-right of content slides (if applicable)

### Reports (DOCX)
- Cover: Dark background, ice blue accent line, Syne Bold title
- Headings: Syne Bold for H1-H2, Work Sans Medium for H3+
- Body: Work Sans 11pt, 1.5 line spacing
- Callout boxes: Brand-50 background, Brand-500 left border
- Tables: Dark header row, alternating neutral-50 stripes

### Email Signature
- Name in Work Sans Medium, title in Work Sans Regular
- Ice blue slash separator
- Contact details in Space Mono

### Business Cards
- Front: Dark background, logo with tagline, ice blue slash accent
- Back: White, contact details, QR code
